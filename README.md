# m1cha3l's PCB Business card V1
You were luck enough to recieve my pcb business card! You can keep it in your wallet and use the built in ruler or solder the needed components and get a nice desk accessories which has a 6x6 NeoPixel MATRIX, 2 buttons, vibration motor for notifications and a BME280 sensor for presure, humidity and temerature and supports Zigbee, Matter, Thread and bluetooth and wifi.

## Building
You can buy the components I have in my bom to build this together! Also I included headers which you can solder to add more sensors for smart home!

## Firmware
I have included a simple firmware which shows m1cha3l (my name) on the leds when you press button number 2 and vibrates when you press button number 1.
1. Open Arduino IDE
2. Choose seeed xiao ESP32-C6
3. Flash and enjoy!!
